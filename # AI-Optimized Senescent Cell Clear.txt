## Executive Summary

Cellular senescence drives aging and age-related diseases through the senescence-associated secretory phenotype (SASP). This whitepaper presents an AI-driven platform that identifies, targets, and eliminates senescent cells with unprecedented precision while preserving healthy cells, enabling comprehensive tissue rejuvenation and healthspan extension.

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 The Senescence Burden
- **Prevalence**: 10-35% of cells in aged tissues
- **Disease Impact**: Contributes to 100+ age-related conditions
- **Economic Cost**: $4.1T annually in US healthcare spending

### 1.2 Senescence Biology
- **Hallmarks**:
  - Cell cycle arrest (p16/p21)
  - SASP (IL-6, MMPs, TGF-β)
  - Mitochondrial dysfunction
  - Lysosomal enlargement (β-gal+)
  - DNA damage foci (γH2AX+)
